Volunteer, my passion! The Inspirational Volunteer

Waker is the first volunteer that inspired me of the essence of community service. In this one-week long program, we designed courses and accompanied the youngsters, and, besides lecturing, we aimed to help them to explore their dreams, interests, and future. Witnessing the children's joyful expression, engaging in late-night group discussion in preparing and reflecting on our courses without feeling exhausting, and experiencing a feeling of belongingness while working with my peers, I finally understood the feeling of being needed by others and how my efforts worth it.
